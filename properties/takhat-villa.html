Welcome to Takhat Villa

Your Hillside Sanctuary

Escape to the Aravallis

Nestled in the verdant hills of Kumbhalgarh, Takhat Villa is a luxury retreat where the wilderness of Rajasthan meets refined comfort. Surrounded by the ancient Aravalli range and just minutes from the majestic Kumbhalgarh Fort, our property offers an experience unlike any other.

With 7 beautifully appointed rooms — 5 Super Deluxe and 2 Premium Suites — a sparkling infinity pool, and a host of curated experiences from jeep safaris to sundowner evenings, Takhat Villa is where memories are made.

Things to Do

Curated Experiences

Swimming Pool

Take a refreshing dip in our stunning pool surrounded by lush greenery, or lounge poolside as the Aravalli hills paint the horizon in golden hues at dusk.

Jeep Safari

Embark on a thrilling jeep safari through the rugged Aravalli terrain. Spot leopards, deer, and exotic birdlife in the wild landscapes surrounding Kumbhalgarh.

Sundowner

Watch the sun melt into the Aravallis with a handcrafted cocktail in hand. Our curated sundowner experience is the perfect way to end a day in paradise.

Hiking & Trekking

Explore scenic trails through the ancient Aravallis. From gentle nature walks to challenging treks leading to hidden waterfalls and panoramic viewpoints.

Bonfire

Gather around a crackling bonfire under a canopy of stars. Share stories, enjoy live folk music, and savor local delicacies as the night comes alive in the hills.

Taste & Savour

Dining at Takhat Villa

A Feast for the Senses

Our in-house restaurant celebrates the rich culinary heritage of Rajasthan while embracing modern gastronomic artistry. Every meal is prepared with fresh, locally sourced ingredients by our talented chefs.

From aromatic Rajasthani thalis and slow-cooked kebabs to continental favorites and healthy breakfast spreads — dine amidst the serene beauty of the Aravallis, whether under open skies or in our warm, inviting indoor space.
