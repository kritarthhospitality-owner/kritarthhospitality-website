About Us

A Legacy of Royal Hospitality

Welcome to Rajput Resort, a heritage stay nestled in the heart of Udaipur — the City of Lakes. Our resort blends centuries-old Rajput tradition with modern luxury, offering guests an experience that echoes the grandeur of Mewar royalty.

Set against the backdrop of the Aravalli hills and overlooking the serene waters of Lake Pichola, every corner of our property tells a story of art, valor, and timeless elegance. From hand-carved jharokhas to sprawling courtyards adorned with fountains, Rajput Resort is more than a stay — it is a journey into Rajasthan's glorious past.

Whether you seek tranquil relaxation, culinary adventures in Rajasthani cuisine, or curated tours of Udaipur's legendary heritage sites, our dedicated staff ensures every moment is steeped in warmth and authenticity.

What We Offer

Our Services

Accommodation

Indulge in our exquisitely appointed heritage suites, each adorned with hand-painted frescoes, antique furnishings, and panoramic views of the Aravalli hills or Lake Pichola.

Dining

Savor authentic Rajasthani cuisine — from the fiery Laal Maas to delicate Dal Baati Churma — prepared by master chefs using recipes passed down through generations of royal kitchens.

Spa & Wellness

Rejuvenate your senses with traditional Ayurvedic therapies, aromatic oil massages, and yoga sessions in our tranquil spa pavilion, set amidst fragrant jasmine gardens.

Local Tours

Explore Udaipur's magnificent heritage — the City Palace, Jag Mandir, Saheliyon-ki-Bari, and sunset boat rides on Lake Pichola — with our expert local guides.
