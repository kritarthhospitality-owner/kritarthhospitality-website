About Us

A Legacy of Royal Hospitality

Welcome to Kritarth Hospitality, a premium hospitality group rooted in the royal city of Udaipur. We curate handpicked properties across Rajasthan, each blending centuries-old tradition with modern luxury for an experience that echoes the grandeur of Mewar royalty.

From the Aravalli hills of Kumbhalgarh to the serene lakes of Udaipur, our properties tell stories of art, valor, and timeless elegance. Every stay is a journey into Rajasthan's glorious heritage — through hand-carved architecture, sprawling courtyards, and landscapes that take your breath away.

Whether you seek tranquil hillside retreats, thrilling jeep safaris, sundowners overlooking the wilderness, or curated heritage tours — our dedicated team ensures every moment is steeped in warmth and authenticity.
